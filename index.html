About

Sometime during the pandemic the stirs of starting a kinky krewe had begun. A logo had been designed from a picture with the help of fellow kinkster Abby Young. And at the end of 2021 after a year or so of discussions we came together and decided to start Krewe de Kink. So here we are in our sophmore year of parading. We hope to show returning and new members a good time on the route and can't wait to meetup for shennanigans whilst creating throws for Boheme parade that happens on February 2nd, 2023. It's a nice walk thru the marigny and quarter.
